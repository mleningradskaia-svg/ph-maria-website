A LITTLE ABOUT

Hi Friend! I'M MARIA

I'm Maria Leningradskaya — a lifestyle and portrait photographer based in Illinois. For the past 7 years, families, couples, women, and men have trusted me to capture the moments that truly matter — whether it's a milestone, a personal portrait session, or photos for a business portfolio.

As a mom of two daughters, I know just how important it is to preserve family memories. I absolutely love working with kids and creating natural, heartfelt portraits.

Photography is more than a job to me — it's my passion. I'm based in Chicago and shoot throughout Illinois, and I'm always open to travel for sessions in other states too.
